Why you should hehehe more in life

You don’t always have to be composed, curated, or quietly impressive. Sometimes life isn’t asking for your “best version”; it just wants you, unfiltered … and a little unhinged :)

We wear so many versions of ourselves like layers, like armor, like performance reviews waiting to happen… but not every moment is an audition. Some are just… pauses. Soft glitches in the system where you’re allowed to drop everything — the act, the expectations, the need to make sense…

Because weirdly enough, it’s never the big, polished highlights that stay. It’s the messy laughter that makes no sense later. It’s the random joy that had no reason to exist. It’s the tiny “haha hehe” moments that slip through all your defenses and remind you that you’re not here to perform life, you’re here to feel it.

So yeah, maybe take life a little less seriously… not because it doesn’t matter, but because you do… beyond the roles, beyond the mask, beyond whatever you think you’re supposed to be.
